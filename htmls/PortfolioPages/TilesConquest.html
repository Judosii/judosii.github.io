This is 'Tiles Conquest', a mobile game for android devices, I made with a group in 2 weeks, the download is here!

This is a tower defense, territory based game! Enemies will try to reduce your territory, but you are able to buy units to extend your territory, or place turrets which will attack nearby enemies!

Working along side the group's Game Programmer and other Game Designer, I have worked on the placement of turrets in-game, dragging and dropping on a correct tile.

This game was made in Unity in 2025, using JetBrains Rider as the code editor, and it was made in C#!

To best save on performance, we opted for a minimalist style, using simple shapes and colours to have the player understand what was dangerous, the tower's attacks, etc.

To quickly test the game, we used 'Unity Remote', an app allowing you to test the game, from the editor, on your phone! this allowed for quicker tests, without having to make a new build for every fix.
