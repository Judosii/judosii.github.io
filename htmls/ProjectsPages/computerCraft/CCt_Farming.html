This script is really easy. just put it in a turtle and watch it go!

Just make sure the farm this turtle is put on is a square, and the turtle starts at the bottom left corner.

It's hard-coded to work from that position, with a rectangular farm shape!

The script can work for any kind of farm; wheat, carrots, potatoes... it also works with some modded crops in mind!

As for crops that are 2 blocks high, it does check below itself if it does not detect a plant.

And finally, the turtle will replant behind himself, after harvesting!
