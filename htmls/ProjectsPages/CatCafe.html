A work in progress, this will be a chill game, where you take care of cats, serve customers, and help cats be adopted by said clients.

This will be made in the Godot Engine, for its incredible 2D capabilities, and there will be no worries about any Unity shenanigan.

It will be made with a close group of friends, and this is an occasion for us to simply have fun making things!

For now, many things have yet to be decided, but we plan on releasing it on itch.io once finished.
